The Smokey Creek Promise

Pure & Unprocessed

Our honey is carefully harvested without heat or excessive filtering, preserving its vibrant flavor, natural enzymes, and all the goodness from the hive.

Ethical Beekeeping

We are committed to sustainable practices and the well-being of our bees, ensuring healthy hives and a thriving local ecosystem.

Regional Flavors

Each jar captures the unique floral essence of North Carolina's diverse landscapes, offering a variety of distinct and delightful tastes.

Our Dedication to the Bees

Smokey Creek Bee Farm is built on a foundation of respect for nature and a genuine love for bees. Owner Justin's journey into beekeeping began over two decades ago, evolving from a keen interest into a profound commitment to sustainable and ethical practices. We believe healthy bees make the best honey, and our methods reflect this core principle.
